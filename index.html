Welcome to Purchase Help

This app is designed for those that need help narrowing down their best purchase. Can't make a decision? Is one thing better than the other? Can't decide? Well this app is for you.

Instructions:

  1. In Attributes column, enter in your attributes/characteristics/qualities that the ideal item will have (yes ideal. We are wishing here and you'll see why).
  2. In the Ranking column, rank each item in the attributes column from 1-5 of how important it is to you. (1 being less important, 5 being crucial to have).
  3. In the Purchase Option column, you'll be comparing one of your purchase options to the categories you've assigned. Give it a score from 1-10 on how that particular item does per each category (1 is not well/doesn't have that attribute, 10 being is the example of that attribute).
